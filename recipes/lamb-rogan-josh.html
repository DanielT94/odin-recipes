Lamb Rogan Josh

The heavenly blend of spices in this rich, aromatic curry combined with meltingly tender lamb pieces is just the ticket for a cold winterâ€™s night.

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Tomato paste
  • 750 g lamb leg, fat trimmed, cut into 2.5cm cubes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 x 5cm piece of ginger, peeled and grated
  • 100 ml natural yoghurt

Directions

  1. Combine spices and tomato paste with a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan and brown the lamb in batches. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, fry the bay, cinnamon, cloves and onion until the onion is soft and translucent. Add the garlic and ginger and fry for a further 2mins. Add the paste and fry for another 2 mins.
  4. Add the lamb and 400ml of water and gently simmer for 1 hr, stirring occasionally.
  5. Discard the bay and cinnamon and stir through the yoghurt, cooking for a further 10 mins.
  6. Sprinkle with freshly chopped coriander before serving.
